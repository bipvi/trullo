 The Property

A centuries-old trullo recently restored with artisan care. Located in open countryside, minutes from the village of San Michele Salentino.

"Every stone detail tells a centuries-old story, lovingly preserved. A space designed for absolute relaxation — where the countryside takes over, and so does your breathing."

Where signal
fades,
life returns.

Mobile coverage is sparse. There is no Wi-Fi. The countryside takes over — and depending on who you ask, that is either the only drawback or the greatest feature of all.

Nature, Unfiltered

Ancient olive groves, cicadas at dusk, dry earth after a summer storm. The trullo sits in open countryside — the nearest neighbour is a stand of centuries-old olives. No notification competes.

A Real Digital Detox

Mobile signal is, let's say, optimistic. Calls may require a short walk outside. There is no Wi-Fi. If you need to be unreachable, you have found the right place.

The Darkest Skies

Zero light pollution means the Milky Way is visible with the naked eye. Lie on the chianca stone of the courtyard and look up. Some things are only possible when you actually switch off.

Good to know before you arrive: Download offline maps (Google Maps or Maps.me), save our number, and let people know you might be hard to reach. The nearest town with strong signal is San Michele Salentino — a few minutes by car.

Private Pool
